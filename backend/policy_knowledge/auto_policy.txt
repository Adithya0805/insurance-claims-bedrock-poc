Auto Policy Coverage Summary — Tamil Nadu Standard Motor Plan

Own Damage Cover: Repairs to the insured vehicle from accidents, collisions,
or third-party impact are covered up to the vehicle's Insured Declared
Value (IDV). Standard claims for bumper, tail lamp, and panel damage
following a documented collision are typically settled within the IDV
without additional scrutiny if under INR 200,000.

Documentation Required: A police FIR is mandatory for third-party
collision claims exceeding INR 50,000 or involving disputed liability.
Claims without a police report attached should be flagged for manual
review before approval.

Exclusions: Damage from racing, driving under the influence, or use of
the vehicle for commercial purposes on a private policy is excluded.
Claims filed more than 30 days after the incident date require a
delay-justification note from the claimant.
