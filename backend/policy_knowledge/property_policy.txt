Property Policy Coverage Summary — Tamil Nadu Homeowner Plan

Water Damage Cover: Damage from rainfall-induced flooding, drainage
overflow, or municipal water ingress is covered up to INR 500,000 per
incident, provided the property has been continuously insured and
occupied for at least 90 days prior to the incident.

Documentation Required: Claims above INR 100,000 require independent
verification of the weather/flooding event (municipal record, news
report, or weather bureau bulletin) plus photographic evidence of
damage. Furniture and electrical fixture claims require itemized
replacement estimates.

Exclusions: Damage from pre-existing structural issues, unmaintained
drainage on the claimant's own property, or gradual seepage over more
than 6 months is excluded and requires a structural engineer's report
before any payout consideration.
