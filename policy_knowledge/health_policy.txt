Health Policy Coverage Summary — Tamil Nadu Family Health Plan

Hospitalization Cover: In-patient hospitalization exceeding 24 hours is
covered up to the policy's annual sum insured, including surgery,
room rent (capped per plan tier), and pre/post-hospitalization expenses
within 30/60 day windows respectively.

Cashless-to-Reimbursement Conversion: If a cashless claim is partially
delayed or denied at the network hospital, the claimant may submit
original bills and discharge summary for reimbursement processing.
These conversions should be prioritized and typically process within
7-10 working days once complete documentation is received.

Exclusions: Cosmetic procedures, non-prescribed treatments, and claims
without a discharge summary or itemized bill are excluded from
reimbursement until documentation is complete.
